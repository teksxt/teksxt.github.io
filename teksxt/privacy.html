Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how Teksxt ("we", "us", "our") handles information in connection with all mobile applications we publish on Google Play, including but not limited to Flip Coin & Dice, 2048, and Sudoku. This single policy covers every Teksxt app currently on Google Play.

In short: We don't collect your personal data directly. Our apps are offline-first where possible. Some apps show ads via Google AdMob, which uses advertising identifiers to serve relevant ads. You can reset or opt out of this at any time from your device settings.

1. Who we are

Teksxt is an independent Android software studio based in India. You can reach us at teksxt71@gmail.com.

2. Apps covered by this policy

This policy applies to every app published by the Teksxt developer account on Google Play. If a future app has different data practices, we will either update this policy or publish a separate app-specific policy and link to it from this page.

3. Information we collect

Directly from you: None. Our apps do not ask you to sign up, create an account, or provide personal information. We do not collect your name, email, phone number, contacts, photos, location, or any similar personal data.

Stored locally on your device: Our apps may save small amounts of data on your device to remember things like best scores, game progress, preferences, and theme choice. This data stays on your device, is never transmitted to us, and is removed when you uninstall the app or clear its storage.

Automatically, via third-party services: Our apps integrate the following third parties, each of which has its own privacy practices:

  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect the Android Advertising ID, device information, IP address, and basic interaction data to serve and measure ads. See Google's ads privacy policy.
  • Google Play Services — required by Android to run the app and deliver crash reports to the developer. See Google's privacy policy.
  • Google Play Billing — if an app offers in-app purchases or a premium upgrade. Payments are handled entirely by Google; we never see your card details.

4. How information is used

We do not use your personal data because we do not collect it. Third parties listed above use the information they collect to serve ads, measure ad performance, detect fraud, ensure service stability, and comply with their own legal obligations. We receive only aggregated, anonymised reports (for example, total ad impressions or crash counts) — never individual user data.

5. Permissions our apps request

Android requires apps to declare permissions. Our apps request only what they need to function:

  • Internet — to load ads and check for updates.
  • Access Network State — to detect whether you're online before attempting to load ads.
  • Vibrate (some games) — for optional haptic feedback on interactions.

We do not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, storage, or any other sensitive permission. If a future app requires a sensitive permission for a specific feature, we will disclose it in the app's Play Store listing and in this policy.

6. Google Play Data Safety declarations

Our current apps declare the following on the Google Play Data Safety form:

  • Data collected by the app: None.
  • Data collected by third parties: Advertising ID and approximate technical device data (via Google AdMob).
  • Data encrypted in transit: Yes — all network traffic uses HTTPS.
  • Users can request data deletion: There is no Teksxt-held data to delete. Local app data can be cleared via Android Settings → Apps → [App Name] → Storage → Clear data. Advertising ID can be reset via Android Settings → Privacy → Ads.

7. Children's privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. Apps that are rated for all ages on Google Play follow Google's Families Policy; when a user is identified by Google Play as a child, non-personalised ads are served in accordance with that policy.

8. Your rights

Because we do not hold personal data about you, there is nothing for us to access, correct, or delete on our side. However, you always have rights in relation to the third parties named above:

  • Reset your Advertising ID or opt out of personalised ads: Android Settings → Privacy → Ads.
  • Request your data from Google: via your Google Account.
  • If you're in the EU / UK (GDPR): you have rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection against any controller processing your data.
  • If you're in India (DPDP Act 2023): you have rights of access, correction, erasure, grievance redressal, and nomination in relation to your personal data.

To exercise any of these rights in relation to Teksxt, email teksxt71@gmail.com with "Privacy request" in the subject line. We aim to respond within 30 days.

9. Data retention and transfers

We do not retain personal data ourselves. Third-party services listed above may transfer data internationally under their own privacy frameworks and retention schedules.

10. Security

We take reasonable measures to keep our build and release pipeline secure. Because our apps run locally on your device and do not communicate with Teksxt servers, there is no Teksxt-held data to breach.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be noted on this page with an updated date at the top. Continued use of our apps after changes constitutes acceptance.

12. Contact

Questions, complaints, or data-rights requests: teksxt71@gmail.com. We aim to respond within 30 days.
