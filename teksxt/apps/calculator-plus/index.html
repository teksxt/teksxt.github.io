Calculator Plus

A clean, everyday calculator with history and unit conversion. No clutter.

Beta: This app is currently in beta testing. Features and behaviour may change before the stable release.

What it does

  • Standard and scientific calculator modes
  • Calculation history — scroll back through previous results
  • Built-in unit converter (length, weight, temperature, currency)
  • Percentage and tip calculator
  • Dark and light themes
  • Works completely offline
