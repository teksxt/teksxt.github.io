Snake Classic — Privacy

This page summarises the data-safety declarations for Snake Classic as listed on Google Play. For the full privacy policy, see the Teksxt master privacy policy.

Google Play Data Safety

  • Data collected by the app: None.
  • Data collected by third parties: Advertising ID and approximate device data (via Google AdMob).
  • Data encrypted in transit: Yes (HTTPS).
  • Users can request data deletion: No Teksxt-held data to delete. Clear local data via Android Settings → Apps → Snake Classic → Storage → Clear data.

Permissions

  • Internet — to load ads.
  • Access Network State — to check connectivity before loading ads.

Data Retention

Local data: Game scores, settings, and preferences are stored only on your device. This data is retained until you uninstall the app or manually clear app data via Android Settings → Apps → Snake Classic → Storage → Clear data. We do not store any of this data on our servers.

Third-party data: Google AdMob may retain advertising identifiers and ad interaction data according to Google's ads data retention policy. Google typically retains ad-related data for up to 24 months. You can reset your Advertising ID at any time via Android Settings → Privacy → Ads.

Crash reports: If the app crashes, Google Play Services may send anonymous crash data to the developer console. This data is retained by Google for up to 180 days and contains no personally identifiable information.

Summary: Teksxt does not retain any personal data. All user data stays on the device. Third-party services (Google AdMob, Google Play Services) retain data according to their own published retention schedules linked above.
