Sensor Toolbox

Accelerometer, gyroscope, compass, and more — all your device sensors in one place.

Beta: This app is currently in beta testing. Features and behaviour may change before the stable release.

What it does

  • Real-time readouts for accelerometer, gyroscope, magnetometer, proximity, light, and pressure sensors
  • Compass with magnetic and true-north heading
  • Graph view for sensor data over time
  • Export sensor readings as CSV
  • Dark and light themes
  • Works completely offline — no data leaves your device

Permissions

This app accesses hardware sensors on your device (accelerometer, gyroscope, magnetometer, etc.) to display real-time data. Sensor readings are processed locally and are never transmitted off your device. See the Sensor Toolbox privacy policy for details.

Legal

This app is covered by the Teksxt privacy policy and the Teksxt terms of service, which apply to every app we publish. It also has an app-specific privacy page covering sensor data handling.
