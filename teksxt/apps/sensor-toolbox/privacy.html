Sensor Toolbox — Privacy

This page summarises the data-safety declarations for Sensor Toolbox as listed on Google Play. For the full privacy policy, see the Teksxt master privacy policy.

Sensor data stays on your device. Sensor Toolbox reads hardware sensors (accelerometer, gyroscope, magnetometer, proximity, light, pressure) to display real-time data. All readings are processed locally and are never transmitted to Teksxt or any third party. Exported CSV files are saved to your device storage only.

Google Play Data Safety

  • Data collected by the app: None directly. Local sensor data is processed on-device only and never transmitted.
  • Data collected by third-party SDKs: Advertising ID and approximate device data (via Google AdMob) for ad serving.
  • Data encrypted in transit: Yes (HTTPS for ad requests).
  • Users can request data deletion: No Teksxt-held data to delete. Clear local data via Android Settings → Apps → Sensor Toolbox → Storage → Clear data.

Permissions

  • Internet — to load ads.
  • Access Network State — to check connectivity before loading ads.
  • Body Sensors (if available) — to access hardware sensor data for display. No data is transmitted.
  • Vibrate — for optional haptic feedback.

Data Retention

Local data: Sensor readings, settings, and preferences are stored only on your device. This data is retained until you uninstall the app or manually clear app data via Android Settings → Apps → Sensor Toolbox → Storage → Clear data. We do not store any of this data on our servers.

Third-party data: Google AdMob may retain advertising identifiers and ad interaction data according to Google's ads data retention policy. Google typically retains ad-related data for up to 24 months. You can reset your Advertising ID at any time via Android Settings → Privacy → Ads.

Analytics data: We do not use any analytics service that retains user-level data. No personal data is transmitted from the app to Teksxt servers.

Crash reports: If the app crashes, Google Play Services may send anonymous crash data to the developer console. This data is retained by Google for up to 180 days and contains no personally identifiable information.

Summary: Teksxt does not retain any personal data. All user data stays on the device. Third-party services (Google AdMob, Google Play Services) retain data according to their own published retention schedules linked above.
